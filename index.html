Direction keys (first match wins)

Click a field and press a key. Multiple keys per direction allowed.

Short beep when you press a Stratagem direction key (keyboard only).

Speed by level

Per level: time for one full code (ms), score per success, points lost on fail, extra time debt subtracted from the next code.

Lives & final screen

0 = unlimited. When the limit is reached, the final screen (defeat) opens.

Classic invasions — on / off & order

Turn factions on or off and choose which invasion is evaluated first when several are ready on the same success. Timing and fog options are in each faction’s section below.

Automaton invasion (classic play)

After enough successful stratagems in a row, a Cyberstan-style “invasion” tint and watermark appear for a random duration.

Illuminate invasion (classic play)

Streak-based Illuminate-style tint, font, and watermark for a random duration; optional blinking alert first. Enable/disable and order are in “Classic invasions” above.

Terminid invasion (classic play)

Streak-based spore fog over the playfield; opacity pulses between configurable levels. Enable/disable and order are in “Classic invasions” above.

General Brasch (classic play)

Every N successful stratagems in a round: larger UI, optional anthem, round timer paused for a few seconds.

Shown large during Brasch mode. You can use a local file under assets/images/ or reference the wiki article for art.

Play & kiosk

Same settings apply in normal play and ?kiosk=1. Cookies mirror the config when it fits browser limits.

Final screen — defeat

Shown when you run out of lives, kiosk lottery / pressure loss, etc. Use {score} in the message. QR encodes the URL you enter (for phones).

QR is generated from this link and shown on the final screen.

Final screen — victory

Shown when a timed kiosk session ends (sprint / marathon). Use {score} in the message.
